~ About Me ~

Hello, my name is Tanish Sharma and I am a second-year BCCA student at Dr. MK Umate Arts and Commerce College in Nagpur.

I have had the opportunity to visit various historical sites such as Jodhpur, Delhi, Jaisalmer, Udaipur, Chittorgarh, Agra, Bhopal, Pokaran, Nagaur, Kuchaman City, the Indo-Pak border, Ellora caves, Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar (Aurangabad), and more.In addition to traveling, I have a passion for drawing, painting, and crafting.I thoroughly enjoy reading books on history and expanding my knowledge in this area.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me.
